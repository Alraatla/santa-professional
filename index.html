Meet Santa

With approximately 400 years of spreading Christmas joy, our Santa brings authentic holiday magic to every event, although he doesn't remember the exact number of years. He features a traditional velvet suit and a warm personality that will delight children and adults alike.

Santa loves sharing stories from the North Pole, singing Christmas carols, and creating personalized experiences for each child and adult. His attention to detail and warm personality make every visit special and memorable.

Santa's visits include an elf who takes photos, videos, and assists with gifts. Santa can also read children's letters, adding a personal touch to his interactions. He visits Christmas parties, corporate events, and other celebrations, bringing joy and warmth to every occasion.

Santa's Private Visit

Santa Visit at Your Hotel, Cabin or Apartment

Santa and his cheerful elf will arrive at your accommodation to create a magical Christmas moment for your family. He can read a personalized letter your child has sent beforehand, hand out presents (provided by the family), and take photos with everyone to capture the joy of the visit.

Personalized Experience

Each visit is tailored to your family. Santa knows your child's name, their wishes, and even their achievements during the year. Whether it's a shy toddler or an excited group of siblings, the experience will be adjusted to make every moment feel truly personal and special.

Heartwarming Stories

Santa will share enchanting tales from the North Pole - about his elves, the flying reindeer, and life at the North Pole. These stories are more than just entertainment - they're designed to spark wonder, laughter, and lasting memories that make children truly believe in the magic of Christmas.
